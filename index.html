About Us

The Cyber Security Lab at Gyeongsang National University is dedicated to researching a safer cyber world. Our goal is to enhance the safety of cyber-physical systems, medical AI, and autonomous driving AI through advanced software engineering.

Meet The Team

The Cyber Safety Lab at Gyeongsang National University is led by Professor Jin Hyun Kim and consists of graduate students pursuing master's and doctoral degrees, as well as undergraduate research assistants. We are organized into three teams: the Medical AI Research Team, the Autonomous Driving AI (AV AI) Research Team, and the Undergraduate Research Team.
