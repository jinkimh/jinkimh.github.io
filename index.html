About Us

The AiX LabLab at Gyeongsang National University is committed to advancing the safety and trustworthiness of modern intelligent systems. We develop methods to ensure the reliability of CPS, medical AI, and autonomous driving AI through formal verification and practical software engineering.

 Research

We conduct research on the safety of software systems.

 Education

We provide education in AI, formal methods, and software engineering.

 Development

We develop medical AI and autonomous driving software for racing.

 Competition

We compete with other teams to build safer and more reliable software.
