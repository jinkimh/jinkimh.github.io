About Us

AiX Lab at Gyeongsang National University advances trustworthy AI through mathematically grounded verification, safe domain-specialized LLMs, clinically meaningful medical intelligence, and real-world autonomous racing platforms.

 Research

We conduct research on the safety of software systems.

 Education

We provide education in AI, formal methods, and software engineering.

 Development

We develop medical AI and autonomous driving software for racing.

 Competition

We compete with other teams to build safer and more reliable software.
