About Us

AiX Lab at Gyeongsang National University advances trustworthy AI through mathematically grounded verification, safe domain-specialized LLMs, clinically meaningful medical intelligence, and real-world autonomous racing platforms.

 Research

We study the safety, reliability, and performance of software and AI systems, from formal specifications to real-world deployments.

 Education

We teach AI, formal methods, and software engineering with hands-on labs, focusing on building trustworthy, system-level thinking.

 Development

We develop medical AI pipelines and autonomous driving stacks for real-world racing and clinical applications.

 Competition

We participate in F1TENTH, RoboRacer, and AI challenges, testing our ideas in competitive and realistic environments.
