AiX Lab – Research Mission & Vision

AiX Lab at Gyeongsang National University advances trustworthy AI through mathematically grounded verification, safe domain-specialized LLMs, clinically meaningful medical intelligence, and real-world autonomous racing platforms.

Our Mission

  • Develop AI systems that are safe, interpretable, and dependable in real-world conditions.
  • Build domain-specialized LLMs that achieve trustworthy reasoning and reduced hallucination.
  • Create clinically meaningful Medical AI for ophthalmology and healthcare decision support.
  • Advance autonomous robot racing and safe high-speed navigation under uncertainty.

Our Vision

We strive to build AI technologies that are:

  • Provably safe through formal verification and runtime assurance.
  • Interpretable and aligned for transparent and accountable decision-making.
  • Clinically useful through noise-robust medical modeling and domain LLMs.
  • Fast and reliable in high-speed autonomous robotics.
  • Educationally impactful by training the next generation of AI and CPS engineers.

Our Key Strengths

  • Formal Methods for AI Safety
    Timed automata, process algebra, and model checking for provably safe and correct AI-driven systems, including verified code generation and runtime monitoring.
  • Safe & Domain-Specialized LLMs
    Compact and trustworthy LLMs (Ophtimus Series) designed for interpretability, alignment, and reduced hallucination with applications in medicine and engineering.
  • Medical AI & Oculomics
    Deep learning and LLM-augmented models for ophthalmology, oculomics, clinical decision support, and noise-robust diagnostic modeling.
  • Robot Racing & Autonomous Driving
    Research and education using F1TENTH and RoboRacer platforms focusing on safe high-speed navigation, SLAM, motion planning, reinforcement learning, and real-world uncertainty handling.
